公司简介 / Company Profile

泽盈国际贸易有限公司（Flowwin International Trading Co., Ltd.）是一家以进出口贸易为核心业务的综合性国际贸易公司，致力于“让优质产品惠及全球，让世界因流动而更丰盈”。公司坚持“以消费者为中心”，连接中国制造与全球美好生活，通过严格甄选与可持续供应链管理，将高品质产品推向世界。

Flowwin is a comprehensive international trading company devoted to bringing quality products to the world and enriching life through flow. Guided by the values of quality, diversity, and mutual prosperity, Flowwin connects Chinese manufacturing with global well-being.

发展历程 / Development Timeline

2005 — 起航 / Foundation

泽盈国际贸易有限公司在中国广东省珠海市成立，注册资本人民币 600 万元。公司以出口针织纺织品与日用轻工产品为主，首年员工 38 人。

Founded in Zhuhai, China with an initial registered capital of RMB 6 million, Flowwin started its journey in textiles and light industrial exports.

2008 — 布局 / Regional Expansion

在深圳与宁波设立首批办事处，业务覆盖东南亚与中东市场。年营业额突破 RMB 50 million。

Opened offices in Shenzhen and Ningbo, expanding exports to Southeast Asia and the Middle East.

2012 — 增资与数字化 / Capital Increase & Digital Upgrade

注册资本由 600 万元增至 1200 万元；员工规模达 200 人。建立 ERP 供应链系统，形成信息化管理框架。

Capital increased to RMB 12 million and ERP systems implemented to enhance supply-chain efficiency.

2015 — 品牌化 / Brand Transformation

推出自有品牌 FlowLiving 与 WinStyle 系列，主攻欧美家居与生活方式市场；进入跨境电商渠道（Amazon、Lazada 等）。

Launched in-house brands and entered cross-border e-commerce platforms to diversify markets.

2018 — 国际布局 / Global Presence

在 香港、曼谷、迪拜设立海外子公司；员工突破 800 人，业务覆盖 40 余国家。

Established subsidiaries in Hong Kong, Bangkok, and Dubai, serving over 40 countries.

2020 — 转型与韧性 / Resilience & Transformation

疫情期间启动数字展贸平台 FlowLink，实现订单线上化；成为多家跨国零售商的稳定供应商。

Launched FlowLink and maintained global supply stability during the pandemic.

2023 — 集团化 / Group Integration

员工 1200+；年营收 RMB 2.8 billion；成立 Flowwin Group Holding，整合品牌、供应链与投资板块。

Formed Flowwin Group Holding; annual revenue exceeded RMB 2.8 billion.

2025 — 共赢未来 / Free Flow, Shared Future

推出可持续供应链战略，推进 ESG 与绿色贸易实践；全球业务版图扩展至 60 个国家。

Launched a sustainability initiative and expanded to 60 countries worldwide.

Summary / 总结： 二十年来，Flowwin 从珠海一家中小外贸公司，成长为拥有全球布局的综合性国际贸易集团。

Over two decades, Flowwin has grown from a local trading firm into a global commerce group rooted in trust, innovation, and flow.
